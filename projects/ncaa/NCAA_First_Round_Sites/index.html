What if Net Ranking and Geography were all that mattered?

The NCAA basketball tournament has eight 1st/2nd round sites, and each site has two "pods" which may feed into any regional site. The best teams are given sites based on what is geographically close. Once each site has been filled by two top teams no other top 16 team can be placed there. Also, top teams potential 2nd round matchups are placed into the same site.

I have taken the straight line distance from home gym to potential 1st/2nd round gym of every team in the top 32 of the Net Rankings (as of 3/3/19) and written this page to show the closest site still available for the top 16 teams. Teams ranked 17-32 are then assumed to match up in the second round with the top 16 evenly. For example #1 vs #32, and #16 vs #17.

Click the button to find out how the sites might change as teams move up or down the rankings.

The distance in miles can only be seen on desktop.
